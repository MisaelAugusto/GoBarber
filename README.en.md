<h2 id="description">📑️ Description</h2>
<p>
  GoBarber is a platform for scheduling times in barber shops that are registered in the system. The user can register in the system in order to offer the barber shop service and register their opening hours, or register in order to search for available times in the barber shops already registered on the platform and make the appointment.
</p>
